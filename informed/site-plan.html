Overview

Purpose

[The purpose of our website is to share information about patient health screenings with the medical community. Facilitating both the patient who does not need to take all the exams and the doctors who can view the exams safely and professionally, respecting data security. For this we use super modern technology that helps doctors to be able to make a diagnosis or prescribe exams with more precision. Information is provided chronologically, forming a base with all patient histories.]

Audience

[Our target audience is physicians who want to have patient exam information quickly and securely. And patients who want to have their exam base registered for future consultations. One of the great advantages is that we will have the entire history of the patients in chronological form.]

Content

Home page

[What is the website about? An online platform that stores all your information about your health exams in one place, generating your medical history. Organizing in a practical and fast way. All your automated exams with laboratories and hospitals registered through your account. Your parents and children with a history of exams, consultations, surgeries, vaccines and all their registered medicines are always updated. Practical, easy and safe. The storage of exams and reports can be at long-term conservation risks. The accumulation of exams generates a series of folders and files that take up space and make it difficult to locate. This service guarantees practicality for those who don't have time to waste, being a safe way to store their reports, ensuring that the doctor can have the quick information that can help start treatments quickly that your health deserves. Website benefits: - End loss of essential files for your health history; - Security guaranteed with access control to confidential information; - Documents saved on a highly professional server with unlimited space. - Guarantee of agility and speed in your queries; - Backups that guarantee the security and integrity of all your information; - Support team to guide you; - Interaction between doctors in possible discussions about the reports; - All clinical reports and exams archived in the cloud! - The customer can add any exam he wants; How the website flow works: - The customer enters our website and registers by entering an email, creating a login/password or using a Gmail or facebook account; - Complete the registration with phone information and address; - Choose the partner laboratories and hospitals in which you have or will take the exams or simply choose the way in which the client will register the exams; - The account is now created and you can access the site; - The main page displays the list of information in chronological order according to the user's choice; - A filter allows the user to quickly find specific topics among the various exams; - The User can choose which doctors will share access to the information.]
