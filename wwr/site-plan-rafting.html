Overview

Purpose

[The purpose of our website is to share information with the community of people who enjoy adventure on the river's currents. Rafting in a safe and professional way, enjoying the pleasures of nature combined with a physical activity that stimulates the whole body and mind. We use the classification system used by the International Rafting Federation (IRF) which is standard around the world. He takes into account that rivers vary in flow and behavior. All rapids must be inspected prior to descent and we share best practices for this activity. We also have the entire line of products, from clothing to technical material needed to practice this sport.]

Audience

[Our target customers are people aged between 20 and 50, with a stable financial situation and interest in adventures involving adrenaline and nature. The profile of our clients and friends are people who have a hard work day during the week and want to take advantage of the weekend to relieve stress and get in harmony with nature. We maintain direct contact through digital media. For your convenience, our website is responsive and can be accessed from any device, such as desktop computers, laptops, tablets and mobile phones. We keep a forum up to date with all happenings, always keeping our customers up to date.]
